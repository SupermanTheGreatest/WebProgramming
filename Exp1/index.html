Assassin’s Creed is a popular action-adventure video game franchise developed by Ubisoft that blends historical settings with stealth-based gameplay and a compelling narrative. The series revolves around the centuries-long conflict between the Assassins, who fight for free will, and the Templars, who seek control over humanity. Players explore richly detailed open-world environments set in different historical periods—such as the Crusades, Renaissance Italy, Ancient Egypt, and Viking-era England—while completing missions that involve combat, parkour, exploration, and assassination. Known for its emphasis on historical accuracy, engaging storytelling, and immersive world design, Assassin’s Creed has become one of the most influential and successful franchises in modern gaming.

Assassin’s Creed is an action-adventure video game series developed by Ubisoft that combines historical fiction with stealth, exploration, and combat-driven gameplay. The franchise centers on an ancient struggle between two secret factions: the Assassins, who defend freedom and individual choice, and the Templars, who believe order and control are necessary for peace. Each installment is set in a meticulously recreated historical era, allowing players to experience famous civilizations and events such as Renaissance Italy, Revolutionary France, Ancient Greece, and Viking-age England. Players take on the role of skilled assassins who use parkour, hidden weapons, and strategic combat to complete missions and uncover secrets of the past. Renowned for its immersive open worlds, strong narrative depth, and attention to historical detail, Assassin’s Creed has established itself as a landmark series in the global gaming industry.
