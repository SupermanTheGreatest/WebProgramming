About TrialBridge

Built for patients,
powered by purpose.

We started TrialBridge because finding a clinical trial shouldn't require a medical degree. Everyone deserves equal access to cutting-edge research — and the chance to shape the future of medicine.

Our mission is simple.

Clinical trials are the engine of medical progress — yet over 80% fail to enrol enough patients, while millions of patients who could benefit never hear about them.

TrialBridge exists to close that gap. We make it radically easy for patients to discover, understand, and participate in the studies that matter — while giving researchers the reach they need to move science forward.

We believe that when patients and researchers find each other faster, everyone wins: better treatments get developed, lives improve, and medicine advances.

What we stand for
Our core values
🤝

Patient First

Every decision we make starts with one question: is this better for the patient? Complexity lives on our side, simplicity lives on yours.

🔒

Radical Transparency

We never obscure risks, hide data, or use dark patterns. Plain language, honest summaries, and zero pressure — always.

⚖️

Equal Access

A retired teacher in rural Maharashtra deserves the same access to breakthrough trials as a patient in a major metro hospital. We're building for both.

🧬

Science-Backed

We work hand-in-hand with IRBs, researchers, and clinical coordinators to ensure every trial listed meets rigorous ethical standards.

🛡️

Privacy by Design

HIPAA-compliant from day one. Your data is encrypted, never sold, and fully yours — you can delete everything with a single click.

💬

Human Support

AI helps you find a match, but real people help you through the process. Our patient advocates are available whenever you need them.

Our Story

Built as a project,
designed with intent.

TrialBridge was created as our Semester 4 Mini Project for Web Programming

Under the mentorship of Nishant Sir, our group took on the challenge of solving a genuine problem in healthcare accessibility — making clinical trials easier to find and participate in.

From the warm colour palette and typography to the floating card animations, scroll transitions, and page layouts; every visual and interactive detail of this website was designed by Shreyas.
