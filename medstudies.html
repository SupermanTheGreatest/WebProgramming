Remote Monitoring of Post-MI Patients via Wearable ECG Devices
A 6-month randomised controlled trial evaluating the clinical outcomes of continuous remote ECG monitoring in patients who have experienced a myocardial infarction in the past 90 days. Comparing standard care versus wearable-assisted follow-up.

Cognitive Decline Patterns in Urban Adults with Type 2 Diabetes
A cross-sectional observational study examining the relationship between glycaemic control and early cognitive decline in urban adults aged 50–75 with a Type 2 Diabetes diagnosis of more than 5 years. Includes neuropsychological testing and MRI imaging.
